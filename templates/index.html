Kandy Preschool provides exceptional care and education in a safe and nurturing environment for children 15 months- 5 years old

Toddler Classroom
(15-24 months)

With our low teacher to child ratio, we are able to provide a high level of teacher interaction with your child. Your toddler will explore, question and discover with age appropriate materials and activities.

2/3 Classroom
(2-3 years)

Your younger preschooler will build on basic concepts such as counting, letter and number recognition and phonemic awareness while having plenty of opportunities to play and improve his/her social skills.

Preschool Classroom
(3-5 years)

Our preschool program prepares your child for the next exciting chapter in his/her educational career…kindergarten! With a combination of teacher-guided small and whole group learning experiences, child-guided activities and free choice centers, your child will strengthen his/her areas of social and emotional, physical, language and literacy, mathematical reasoning, social studies, science and creative developments.
