Miss Pam, Owner and Executive Director
Team member since 2010

Hi! My name is Pam Hall and I am the owner and Executive Director of Twinkling Stars Preschool. I opened TSP’s Preschool Classroom in 2010 and added the Toddler and 2/3s Classrooms in 2018. We have loved being a part of the Roscoe Village community! Being the oldest of 5, my love for working with children started at a very young age. From helping my mom with my siblings, to babysitting children in my neighborhood to ultimately my career as teacher; my passion has always been, and will forever be, caring for and teaching little ones. I earned my B.S. in Elementary Education from Northern Illinois University and hold a current Professional Educator’s License. Prior to opening TSP, I taught at a private academy in downtown Chicago, was an assistant teacher in the Tuition-Based Preschool Program at Audubon Elementary School and substitute taught for multiple CPS schools. My husband and I have 3 children and 2 dogs. I love my job, the teachers with whom I work and all the families who have let me be a part of their lives! Thank you for making my dreams come true!
